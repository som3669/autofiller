# AutoFiller — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-17_

AutoFiller is built to keep your data private. It runs entirely on your device.

## What data AutoFiller collects

**None.** AutoFiller does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data. There
are no analytics, no tracking, and no external servers.

## What AutoFiller stores (on your device only)

The information you enter yourself:

- Your profile fields (name, email, phone, address, company, website)
- Your custom field rules

This is saved via Chrome's `chrome.storage.sync`, which may sync it across your
own signed-in devices under your Google account. AutoFiller itself never sees or
transmits this data.

## Passwords

AutoFiller never stores or fills passwords. Password input fields are always
skipped.

## Permissions and why they are needed

- **storage** — to save your profile and custom rules on your device.
- **activeTab** — to access the current tab only when you click Fill.
- **scripting** — to inject the one-off filling script into the current tab when
  you trigger it.
- **contextMenus** — to add the right-click "Fill this page" option.

AutoFiller requests no broad host permissions and only interacts with a page when
you explicitly ask it to fill.

## Data sharing

AutoFiller shares no data with anyone. There is nothing to share.
