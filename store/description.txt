Tired of retyping the same details into every form? AutoFiller fills web forms in one click — using a profile you set up once. Your name, email, phone, and address go in instantly, so checkout pages, sign-ups, and applications take seconds.

AutoFiller does nothing until you tell it to. Click the button, press the shortcut, or use the right-click menu, and it fills the current page. Nothing runs in the background, and it requests no broad access to your browsing.

★ WHY AUTOFILLER

• One click fills the whole form
• Set your details once, reuse everywhere
• Works with modern React and Vue forms
• No passwords ever stored or filled
• Nothing runs until you act — private by design

★ FEATURES

• Save a profile: name, email, phone, full address, company, website
• Fill on click, with Alt+Shift+F, or from the right-click menu
• Custom field rules — match any field by its name, label, placeholder, or a CSS
  selector, and give it a value
• Scope a rule to one site or apply it everywhere
• Smart field detection using autocomplete attributes, input types, and labels
• Framework-safe filling that React and Vue forms recognize

★ PASSWORDS

AutoFiller never stores or fills passwords. Password fields are always skipped.

★ PRIVACY

Everything stays on your device. AutoFiller sends no data anywhere. Your profile
is saved with Chrome's own storage and never leaves your browser.
