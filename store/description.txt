Testing a form? Fill it instantly with one click. AutoFiller populates every field on the page with realistic fake data — names, emails, phone numbers, dates, numbers, dropdowns, checkboxes, and more — so developers and QA can test forms in seconds instead of typing junk by hand.

Need real data instead? Save your own profile and fill with that in one click too.

AutoFiller does nothing until you tell it to. Click the button, press the shortcut, or use the right-click menu — it only touches a page when you ask, and requests no broad access to your browsing.

★ TWO MODES

• Fake data (default) — fills the whole form with realistic random values for testing
• My profile — fills name, email, phone, address, company from data you save

★ FIELD TYPES COVERED

Text, email, tel, number (respects min/max), URL, date, month, week, time, color, range, textarea, select dropdowns, radio buttons, and checkboxes. Field length limits are respected.

★ FEATURES

• Fill on click, with a keyboard shortcut, or from the right-click menu
• Realistic fake data that passes most form validation
• Custom field rules — match any field by name, label, placeholder, or CSS selector
• Smart detection using autocomplete attributes, input types, and labels
• Framework-safe filling that React and Vue forms recognize

★ PASSWORDS

AutoFiller never stores or fills passwords. Password fields are always skipped.

★ PRIVACY

Everything stays on your device. AutoFiller sends no data anywhere. Any profile you save is stored with Chrome's own storage and never leaves your browser.

Perfect for developers and testers who fill forms all day.
